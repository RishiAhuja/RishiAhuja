I design and build scalable systems — from mobile interfaces to backend infrastructure and production-grade platforms that handle real-world load.

Currently building an Agri-Tech venture at **iHub-AWaDH (IIT Ropar)** as an Entrepreneur in Residence, where I’m responsible for shaping the technical foundation, infrastructure strategy, and long-term architecture of agricultural intelligence systems. My work spans identifying product-market fit, defining system roadmaps, and translating research into scalable, deployable products.

Previously, I worked on optimizing ML pipelines on HPC infrastructure at Annam.ai, managing large-scale training workloads on H100 GPUs and improving pipeline efficiency. I’ve also shipped high-impact features at Stack Wealth (YC S21) as a top contributor, delivering production systems used by thousands of daily users.

My focus lies in **architecture-first engineering**, performance optimization, and building systems that remain maintainable as they scale. I enjoy solving problems that sit at the boundary of product, systems, and infrastructure.
